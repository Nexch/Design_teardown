PERSON OF THE WEEK

Aga Naplocha is a Visual Designer and Front-End Developer at Adobe. She loves to share her knowledge and experience and, as a co-founder of the non-profit organization The Awwwesomes, she organizes free HTML and CSS workshops and meetups in Warsaw in which designers and developers learn to create awesome things on the web. You can find her on Twitter as @aganaplocha. Thank you for sharing and caring, dear Aga!
